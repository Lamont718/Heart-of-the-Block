# Heart of the Block — Visual Reference Board

Curated, cited references to push the design from "warm & generic" toward
"warm & unmistakably Brooklyn/Caribbean." Generated 2026-06-22 from a verified
research pass. Items marked **✓** were source-verified; **⚠︎** were surfaced but
not fully verified (the research hit a usage limit) — eyeball before relying on.

The single clearest direction: **"Warm Brooklyn kitchen table"** — cream paper +
brick/gold/teal, **real neighborhood photography as the hero** (not cartoon
illustration), one subtle quilt/madras-derived pattern as connective tissue, and
free spot illustration until commissioned art is feasible.

---

## 1) Black-owned / culturally-grounded wellness brands
- **✓ BLK + GRN** — https://blkgrn.com/ — Black-owned wellness marketplace,
  "More Than a Marketplace. A Movement." Borrow: warm candid lifestyle photography
  of Black people in cozy home/community settings; centering melanated skin tones.
  Closest model for *tone*.
- **✓ Golde** — https://golde.co/ — Black-woman-founded superfood brand. Borrow the
  warm, conversational, non-medicinal voice ("love in a cup"). NOTE: it is bright/
  colorful, NOT earthy — borrow the voice, not the palette.
- **⚠︎ Topicals** — Black-owned skincare that made a health condition playful, not
  clinical (tie-dye, blurred outlines). Proof a health topic can be warm and fun.

## 2) Caribbean & Afro-diasporic visual language
- **⚠︎ Madras plaid** — national-dress textile of St. Lucia, Martinique, Guadeloupe,
  Dominica. Reference respectfully as a subtle abstracted accent, not a flag overlay.
- **⚠︎ Temi Coker** — vibrant collage illustrator rooted in West African/Yoruba
  pattern & color. https://www.papermag.com/temi-coker-feature-2647217399.html
- Motif traditions (designer knowledge, unverified): **Adinkra** symbols (Ghanaian,
  meaning-bearing — good for iconography), **Gee's Bend quilting** (Black-American
  improvisational geometry — warm dividers/backgrounds), **West Indian Day Carnival**
  color energy (Brooklyn-specific!).

## 3) Illustration sources (Black people, warmly)
- **✓ Black Illustrations** — https://www.blackillustrations.com/ — licensable, 30+
  packs incl. Healthcare, Black Motherhood, Black Hair Days. Best for licensed spot art.
- **✓ Loveis Wise** — https://www.loveiswise.com/ — pro illustrator, Black joy/
  community/liberation. For a commissioned hero piece.
- **✓ Open Peeps** — https://www.openpeeps.com/ — hand-drawn mix-and-match characters
  (curly hair + skin-tone customizable), CC0/free for commercial use. Best FREE quick
  win. (Prefer over Humaaans, which lacks real skin-tone controls.)

## 4) Authentic photography sources
- **✓ Nappy.co** — https://nappy.co/ — "Beautiful photos of Black and Brown people,
  for free," commercial use OK. #1 free photo source.
- **✓ Iwaria** — https://www.iwaria.com/ — free African photography. VERIFY exact
  license before commercial use.
- **⚠︎ CreateHER Stock** (https://createherstock.com/), **POC Stock**
  (https://www.pocstock.com/), guide: https://blackcreative.co/where-to-find-authentic-black-stock-photography-video-and-illustration-in-2026/

## 5) Food / community design to emulate
- **⚠︎ Sweetgreen** (COLLINS rebrand) — warm, handmade, "food-as-hero." Make the food
  the visual hero. https://wearecollins.com/case-studies/sweetgreen/
- **⚠︎ Diaspora Co.** — https://www.diasporaco.com/ — cream + vibrant reds/golds,
  natural-light hands-holding-ingredients photography. Palette nearly identical to
  ours — strong layout reference.

## 6) Concrete direction (building on brick/gold/teal + Bricolage/Inter)
- **Palette:** keep the core. Add deep **plum/aubergine** (~#5B2A4A) for rich contrast
  and a deeper **leaf green** (~#3E6B3A) alongside teal for "produce/fresh." Cream stays.
- **Type:** pairing is good. No third face unless a sparingly-used hand/script accent.
- **Pattern/texture:** ONE connective motif — a Gee's-Bend-quilt / abstracted-madras
  band at low opacity for section dividers, footer, card headers. Never behind body
  text. Credit the tradition on the About page. (Shipped as the `.weave-band` CSS strip.)
- **Imagery rule:** photography-first, illustration-second. Real candid Brooklyn people
  + food in natural light (Nappy / BLK+GRN). Open Peeps / Black Illustrations where no
  photo exists. Ban clinical/stocky imagery.
